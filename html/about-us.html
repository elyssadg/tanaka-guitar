About TANAKA Guitar

Our Story

Tanaka Guitar is an all-in-one guitar store in Indonesia. Founded by Mr. whoooooo or known as ‘Bapak Whoooooo’ in Indonesia. It’s all started in August 2021, where Tanaka Guitar is still a small guitar store in Indonesia. And over time, it has become one of Indonesia's most popular guitar shops. Tanaka Guitar has many locations in Indonesia, including Jakarta, Yogyakarta, Surabaya, Denpasar, and Pontianak. Tanaka Guitar has everything a musician could possibly need, whether they're a beginner, an intermediate, or a main stage rocker. What You Can Expect

When you visit one of our stores, you'll discover not only an unrivaled selection of guitars, gear, and accessories, but also how enthusiastic our staff is about guitars, playing guitar, and all things musical. We even sorted the highest-quality guitars for selling in our shop. Come into one of our stores and try out our instruments to get a sense of and feel for each guitar we have on offer so you can find the one that's right for you; but be warned – you may need an entire afternoon because you'll undoubtedly become engrossed in a discussion about your favorite guitars, riffs, artists, and the list goes on... because we love what we do and we love sharing it with you. We'll even show you how to choose your own guitar, including string types, guitar body types, wood types, and even the best price for you. We'll even build you a custom guitar just for you.
