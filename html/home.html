What's On

Categories

Classic Guitar

The classical guitar (also known as the nylon-string guitar or Spanish guitar) is a member of the guitar family used in classical music and other styles.

Electric Guitar

An electric guitar is a guitar that produces sound by vibrating strings over a pickup that converts the vibrations into electrical signals.

Bass Guitar

A bass guitar is the lowest-pitch member of the guitar family. It produces sound when its metal bass strings vibrate over one or more magnetic pickups.

Acoustic Guitar

An acoustic guitar is an instrument in the string family. When a string is plucked, its vibration is transmitted from the bridge, resonating throughout the top of the guitar.

Silent Guitar

A silent guitar is a type of guitar with a solid or chambered body that converts the vibration of the strings into electric current using a piezoelectric pickup.

Amplifier

A guitar amplifier or amp is an electronic device or system that strengthens the weak electrical signal from a pickup on a guitar.
